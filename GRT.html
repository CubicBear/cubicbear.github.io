Geometric Representation Thery Seminar

Organizers: Rui Xiong, Pengcheng Li, Quan Situ and Yaolong Shen.

The seminar participants are mainly young researchers from China working on geometric representation theory. Usually, the series of our seminar is mainly topic seminars mixed with several research talks. To join us, contact any of organizers.
